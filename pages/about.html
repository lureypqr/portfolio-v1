Whoami

I'm a fourth-year BSc student majoring in Physics at Tribhuvan University, Kathmandu. My work sits at the intersection of mathematical physics and data science — I care deeply about understanding why things work, not just running code that happens to produce results.

My work is centered on strengthening fundamentals in physics, mathematics, and programming through continuous study and small-scale projects. I approach problems analytically and try to build clear, reproducible solutions using computational methods. My goal is to develop the ability to connect theory with implementation and apply that skill set to meaningful problems in research or applied data science.
